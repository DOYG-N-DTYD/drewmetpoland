A pallet blank

A pallet blank is a set of bars and boards designed for the assembly of pallets. Wooden pallets are widely used all over the world as containers and packaging for all types of products. Their advantage is 100% environmental compatibility and inexpensive price with high quality and ease of use. Due to their strength and ease of transportation, wood pallets are an inescapable consumable in the warehouses of the companies.

Here you can order pallet blanks of a standard size for the production of the pallets, such as:

  • Euro pallet 1-6 (EPAL, EURO pallets)
  • Chemical pallets (CP)
  • Disposable pallets
  • Dusseldorf pallet

It is also possible to manufacture and supply blanks of special sizes, according to your technical specifications.

Blanks can be natural moisture (wet) and and machine-dried wood in drying chambers up to 20% moisture (KD)

As an option we carry out antiseptic treatment.
