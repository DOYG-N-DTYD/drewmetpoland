Split firewood

Split firewood
is used all over the world as an affordable, environmental compatibility and renewable energy and heat resource.
The most calorific species, emitting the greatest amount of heat, are hard-leaved such as - ash, hornbeam, oak, birch. Wood of these species burns longer and gives a higher heat. The price for firewood from these species is higher, however, they are the most demanded due to their high efficiency.
