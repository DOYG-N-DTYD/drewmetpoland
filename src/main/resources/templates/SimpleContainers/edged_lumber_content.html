Bar Bar (dry or natural moisture edged) lumber with a square or rectangular section, has a thickness of 10 cm and a width less than double thickness. A bar is a sawn log from 4 sides to a square

Dry edged or natural moisture board The board has a thickness of no more than 10 cm and a width of two or more times its thickness. The edged board has flat surfaces on four sides, but in some cases, the wane edge on board set by the norms (part of the uncut side surface) is allowed.

Bar - a variant of a bar with a thickness of less than 100 mm. Bars are used in decoration as a basis for laying floor, ceiling and wall coverings, used in the manufacture of entrance and interior doors.
