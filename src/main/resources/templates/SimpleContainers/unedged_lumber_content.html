Unedged board of natural moisture or machine-dried has a length from 2 to 6 meters. Width and thickness - according to the customer's requirements. It is possible to process unedged boards with special protective impregnations for sea transportation. Unedged lumber is of lower quality, but its low price makes it one of the most affordable building materials, which makes it widely used in many areas. For example, for timbering when pouring a foundation, for strengthening the walls in pits and other construction-related works, for the construction of temporary warehouses, sheds, fences and utility rooms.
