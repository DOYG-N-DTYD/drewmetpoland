Slab / Sawdust

The slab sawn off during the production of bars and boards is a part of the tree, in fact, waste production. It has a low price and is divided, depending on the application, into wood-burning (goes for kindling) and business (used in construction where appearance is not important). Sawdust (Fraction out of disc machines). Suitable for a large number of different variants of usage: such as an absorber (moisture absorber) of oil waste, from old sawdust produce a substrate for the soil, for storing vegetables and fruits on the balcony, in the cellar, as a means of pest control (the pine smell repels), you can cover plants from the freeze in the garden, as a material for noise absorption.
